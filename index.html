Month 1: Foundation Building

The first month focuses on establishing a solid foundation and creating consistent study habits.

Goals:

  • Establish a daily study routine
  • Develop basic listening comprehension
  • Build confidence with simple reading materials
  • Begin regular journal writing
  • Practice pronunciation through reading aloud

Weekly Breakdown:

Weeks 1-2: Listening and Reading Focus
  • Listening: 2 dalilk4english.com podcast episodes weekly
  • Reading: 15 minutes daily on LingQ with beginner-level content
  • Writing: Simple daily journal (3-5 sentences)
  • Speaking: Read aloud podcast transcripts for 5 minutes daily
Weeks 3-4: Speaking and Writing Introduction
  • Listening: Continue with 2 podcast episodes weekly
  • Reading: Increase to 20 minutes daily on LingQ
  • Writing: Daily journal (5-7 sentences) + summarize one podcast episode
  • Speaking: Shadow podcast speakers for 10 minutes daily
